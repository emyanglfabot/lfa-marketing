Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Peek A Boo ("we," "us," or "the app") is made for families with children under 13. We take your family's privacy seriously and designed the app with that in mind. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — written in plain language, not legalese.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Peek A Boo is listed in the Apple App Store Kids category and is intended for children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

A parent or legal guardian must create and manage all accounts. Children cannot create accounts on their own. We do not knowingly collect personal information from a child without verified parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately at hello@landfaraway.co and we will delete it promptly.

What We Collect

We collect only what is needed to make the app work for your family:

  • Child profiles: First name and age (or birthday). Parents create these profiles to personalize the experience.
  • Face photos: Photos of family members used to personalize in-app content. These are stored on your device. If you choose to enable cloud sync, they are also uploaded to our servers.
  • User-generated content: Videos, drawings, and images created in the app. These are stored locally on your device. If cloud sync is enabled by a parent, they may also be synced to our servers.
  • Authentication information: If you sign in, we store your Apple ID token (via Apple Sign-In) or your email address (via magic-link sign-in). We never store passwords.

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or any information beyond what is listed above. We do not use advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking of any kind.

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize the app experience for your child (e.g., showing their name and familiar faces).
  • To let parents manage child profiles, review content, and control cloud sync.
  • To authenticate parents when they sign in.

We do not sell, rent, or share your family's data with third parties — ever.

Apple Sign-In & Email Authentication

Parents can sign in using Apple Sign-In or an email magic link. With Apple Sign-In, Apple provides us with a secure identity token — we do not receive or store your Apple ID password. With email sign-in, we send a one-time verification code to your email address; no password is created or stored.

Cloud Sync & Storage

All content is stored locally on your device by default. Parents can optionally enable cloud sync in the app's Settings. When cloud sync is turned on, child profiles, face photos, and user-generated content are uploaded to our servers hosted on Railway (US-based infrastructure).

Parents have full control over cloud sync and can turn it off at any time in Settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data only as long as your account is active and cloud sync is enabled. You can delete your family's data at any time:

  • On-device data: Delete the app or remove individual child profiles and content from within the app.
  • Cloud data: Turn off cloud sync in Settings to stop uploading. To request full deletion of cloud-stored data, email us at hello@landfaraway.co and we will remove all data associated with your account within 30 days.

Parental Controls

Parents create and manage all accounts and child profiles. Access to Settings, cloud configuration, and account management is protected by a parental gate. Parents can:

  • Add, edit, or delete child profiles.
  • Enable or disable cloud sync.
  • Review and approve, reject, or delete any user-generated content.
  • Delete their account and all associated data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all cloud communication and secure token-based authentication.

Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and notify parents through the app where appropriate.
